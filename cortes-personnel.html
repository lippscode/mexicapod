Cortés Personnel

Gómara reports just before departing Cuba: 550 Spaniards, ~50 sailors, 200 Cuban islander porters, several Negroes, a few Indian women, 16 horses. Díaz counts at Cozumel: 508 men (not counting pilots/captains/sailors ~100), 16 horses, 11 ships, 13 musketeers, 32 crossbowmen, 10 brass guns, 4 falconets (Díaz p. 58–59). *Asterisk denotes persons first mentioned after Narváez's men joined Cortés.
Sources: Bernal Díaz del Castillo · Francisco López de Gómara · Hernán Cortés (Letters) · Francisco de Aguilar · Fernando de Alva Ixtlilxóchitl · Diego Durán · Bernardino Vázquez de Tapia
